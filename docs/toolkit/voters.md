# For Voters

## Know Your Rights

18 USC 592 protects every voter from military or armed federal law enforcement
intimidation at polling places. This federal protection has existed since the
Reconstruction era.

## What You Can Do

1. **Contact your city council** — Ask them to adopt a local election protection ordinance
2. **Report intimidation** — Document any armed federal presence at polling locations
3. **Join the coalition** — Connect with local election protection organizations
