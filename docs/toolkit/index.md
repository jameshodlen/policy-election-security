# Toolkit

Resources for implementing municipal election protection ordinances.

## Who Is This For?

- **[Voters](voters.md)** — Understand your rights and how to advocate for local protections
- **[Organizers](organizers.md)** — Campaign materials, model ordinance language, coalition building guides
- **[Elected Officials](officials.md)** — Legal analysis, model resolutions, and implementation guidance

## Three-Tier Compliance Architecture

The campaign provides three levels of support for organizations navigating 50-state lobbying registration:

| Tier | Service | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **DIY Intelligence** | Self-service tools | State requirement databases, deadline trackers, template filings |
| **Managed Filing** | Professional services | Outsourced registration, reporting, and compliance management |
| **Emergency Legal** | Insurance product | Rapid-response legal support for enforcement actions or challenges |
