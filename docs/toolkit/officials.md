# For Elected Officials

## Why Adopt a Local Election Protection Ordinance?

Municipal ordinances reinforcing 18 USC 592 create a **local civil enforcement mechanism**
for a federal criminal statute that has seen minimal federal enforcement.

## Legal Authority

Your authority to adopt such an ordinance depends on your state's home rule framework.
See [State Authority](../legal-foundation/state-authority.md) for the legal analysis
and your [State Guide](../state-guides/index.md) for jurisdiction-specific assessment.
